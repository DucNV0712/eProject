Largest hang glider in Vietnam at Sun World Danang Wonders!

Vietnam's leading park Sun World Danang Wonders (under the Sun World entertainment brand system, Sun Group) in Da Nang launches a new game Queen Cobra - Vietnam's largest hang glider. Queen Cobra offers a thrilling experience that satisfies adventure enthusiasts when coming to the park.

Established as the largest hang glider in Vietnam with a track length of up to 689m and with 20 seats, Queen Cobra is on the list of the world's top adventure games, inspired by the image of a Snake. king cobra is over 5 meters long in Kerala India.

Within 96 seconds, the twisting rails will give visitors an unprecedented excitement. Queen Cobra promises to bring explosive moments, satisfying adventurous followers who want to discover and conquer themselves.
