Paradise Fall

As the first air roller coaster train model in Vietnam, Paradise Fall consists of 7 trains, each of which has two stylish all-terrain motors, players are thrusted into different sensations from within with the “midair race” that stretches 377m and features various 180-degree twists. On the race that can accelerate up to maximum 58kph, Paradis Fall players are draped in a roller coaster of feelings from the ease at the starting line to fierce speedup on midair bends and softer, more relaxing relief as the train is about to hit the finish line.
Minimum height: 130cm (children standing 120cm to 129cm tall must be chaperoned)
