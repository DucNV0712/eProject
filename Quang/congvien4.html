Queen Cobra is elaborately designed with a suspended roller coaster train that carries players to the peak of 34m and falls hard at the speed of 80kph after flashing over 5 continual 360-degree twists of the rail. Minimum height of players is 130cm and maximum height 195cm . Registered as the Largest Suspended Roller coaster train of Vietnam, the railroad spans up to 689m and can accommodate 20 players each. Within 96 seconds, the wild ride and twists will push all players to incredible boundaries of sensations. Type: Hyper extreme.
