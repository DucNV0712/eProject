Amusement park on water

Description: From June 1, 2019, Sun World Danang Wonders
officially open an amusement park on water, with many relaxing
games such as duck riding, boat trips on the lake bringing
visitors completely new experience. Visitors will be able to
ride a duck boat by themselves around the lake, sightseeing, talking and
checking online at the same time. This will be a relaxing, romantic
experience for young people,
couples or small families every afternoon.
– Ticket price: 50,000 VND/ game/ 30 minutes
– Capacity: maximum 2 people for each game.
– Regulations: + Adults: from 140 cm can join the game
+ Children: from 120-140 cm must be accompanied by an adult.
+ Below 120 cm are not allowed to play
